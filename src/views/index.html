MISSION

Vita Health Center is an Acupuncture Clinic that provides excellent service to our community. We want to help our community to use alternative medicine to prevent or treat health conditions by using different techniques such as needle, moxa, cupping, gua sha, electroacupuncture and others. Our specializations are facial acupuncture, rejuvenation acupuncture, neuro acupuncture, sport & orthopedic acupuncture, fertility, and massage therapy. Our clinic is proud to do a private label on Vita skincare because we want to make sure we can give our clients vegan and organic skincare products and better cosmetic acupuncture results.

VISION

We strive to work with the community to improve the lives of our patients and their families. We provide Acupuncturist and Oriental Medicine expertise to our community as well as other health professionals. We work professionally with our referring Doctors and Acupuncturist to help as needed. We work with conventional doctors and incorporate western medical practices to ensure the best outcome for our patients. We use primarily Vita Skincare products.
